This is the H.P.H

What is the H.P.H?

H.P.H means High Places Helper. We made this for person who cannot walk or stand because

if they cannot stand, they cannot reach things wich are at high places.

How to use it?

If you pull the string which has front of you, the shelf will go up, and if you pull

the another string, the shelf will go down.
